Acakki 19:1 Lumalaika aryo gubino i Codom otyeno; gunoŋo Lot obedo piny i dog gaŋ me Codom. Ka Lot onenogi, ci oa malo, ocito ka jologi, oryebbe, okulo waŋe piny i ŋom,
Acakki 19:2 kun wacci, "Luditona, alegowu, wulokke, wudwog kany, wudony i oda an laticwu, wubed iye nino acel, wulwok iye tyenwu, ci doŋ wubia giwu odiko con, wucito i yo wotwu." Gugamo ni, "Pe; piny biru ki wan i dye gaŋ, kany."
